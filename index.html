About Me

Designing Textiles with Purpose, Creativity & Sustainability

Designing textiles is more than creating patterns it is about shaping experiences through colour, texture, and material. My practice focuses on surface design, print development, and sustainable innovation, transforming research and creative exploration into thoughtfully crafted textile collections.


As a Textile Design postgraduate, I am passionate about creating designs that balance creativity, functionality, and environmental responsibility. Through research, experimentation, and hands-on making, I explore meaningful textile surfaces that connect traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.
